$ About Me $

My name is Cheryl, a junior student currently studying in the Waldorf School of Garden City. I'm from Urumqi, a quiet and relatively small city in China. But I love to travel around, which broadened my horizons, and enriched my knowledge. Through traveling, I got a chance to have a look at different places and experienced their colorful cultures and customs. These wonderful experiences indirectly but enormously influenced my mind. Consequently, those charming signal works of architecture from these places I have been to gradually formed my mind of focusing the direction of majoring in architecture specifically.





$ Study Experience over the World $

I stayed in my home city from kindergarden till 9th grade, and being educated under traditional Chinese education systems. I learned piano and Gu-Qin(a type of Chinese musical instrument) during that period of time. (In the meantiem, I felt like I was more talented and also more interested in visual art. So I started to learn some sorts of Anomies, handcrafts, painting and sketching in the studio with my art teacher since 6th grade.) During the summer break of 7th and 8th Grade, I went to Britain, and meanwhile experiencing in their school for one month. After that, my mother decided to let me study abroad for high school then. So I came to Arizona as an exchange student for one year length in 10th grade. Everything there was great except the climate and weather was extremely dry. Consequently, I switched school to Waldorf(the school I am in right now) in Long Island, New York.
